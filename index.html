Stand Together Against Bullying

The aim and mission of our campaign is to create awareness on bullying in schools, to show how devastating it's effects can be, and to put strategic measures in place to prevent this intimidation from occurring

About Our Hotel

What Do We Do?

Bullying can have a major impact on a child's emotional and mental state. Our goal is to create a safe space for victims as well as the perpetrators. Children who are being bullied are often afraid to report the abuse, be it verbal or physical. Our team is willing to go the extra mile to make them feel comfortable to come forward and seek the help that's needed to deal with their trauma. By speaking to the bullies we can understand why they want to cause harm to other children and find a way to move past this phase
