How To Donate

Donations can be made to our course by attending our fundraiser that is held every second Saturday, to find out more information about this you may visit our Instagram or Facebook pages as all the details about our work will be uploaded on those platforms. If you would like to make a larger sum of donation please contact one of our friendly team members and we will assist you. We truly appreciate your support in making a difference!
